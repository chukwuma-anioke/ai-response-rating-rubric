## Item 04 — Great Wall visibility

**Candidate scores:** 3 vs 2

**The problem:** Two claims, one prominent and false (naked-eye visibility from space), one roughly defensible (13,000+ miles covers the total length of all wall sections). Is the false claim "central" enough to force a 2?

**Resolution:** The question asked specifically about visibility, so the false claim answers the question and the true claim is supporting. That argues for 2. But the visibility claim is a widely repeated cultural belief rather than an invention, and the answer's factual content is genuinely mixed. Scored 3 with both flags. Noted for consistency: any future item where the myth directly answers the question should also get 3, not 4.
